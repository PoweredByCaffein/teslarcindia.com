IN BRIEF ABOUT US

TESLARC is a Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) practicing engineering solution company. At TESLARC, we provide solutions to engineering problems in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics and Computational Structural Mechanics.

We have a team of technically strong domain experts and we offers services in the field of aerospace, automobile, marine, turbo-machinery and HVAC. We can provide solutions and take up R&D work from the various industries and government organizations. 

OUR VISION

To provide quality solutions to the engineering problems and at the same time which is benefitting to the engineering community and the society as a whole. 

OUR MISSION

  • Address CFD problems with great care and provide accurate solutions.
  • Investigate complex engineering problems of industries and society and contribute by providing solutions to them.
  • Develop customized solutions through modeling and simulation.
  • Develop solutions through experts at TESLARC and by partnering with customer.
  • Delivering what is committed within scheduled deadline.
  • Contribute through innovative products and solutions for the development of industries and societal issues.
